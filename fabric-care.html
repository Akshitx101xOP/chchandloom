Longevity Guide

Caring for Your
Handcrafted Textiles

The Act of
Care

At CHC Handlooms, we believe that the life of a fabric is extended through intentional maintenance. Our handcrafted cotton is designed to age gracefully, becoming softer and more tactile with time. By following a few simple steps, you can ensure that your affordable furnishings remain a cherished part of your home for years. Visit us at Sarojini Nagar Market, New Delhi.

Gentle Washing

Always use a gentle machine cycle or hand wash in cold water. Avoid harsh detergents or bleach, which can strip the natural fibers of their softness and vibrancy.

Natural Drying

We recommend air drying your textiles in a shaded area. While our cotton loves fresh air, direct and prolonged exposure to harsh sunlight can fade colors over time. Avoid tumble drying to prevent shrinkage.

Mindful Ironing

If you prefer a crisp finish, iron your cotton while it is still slightly damp using a medium-heat setting. This helps the fibers settle into their natural, smooth state.

Storage

Store your furnishings in a cool, dry place. Using breathable cotton bags for storage (rather than plastic) allows the fabric to maintain its freshness and prevents any moisture buildup.
