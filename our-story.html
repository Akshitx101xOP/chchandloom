Our Heritage

Rooted in the
Textile Heart of Delhi

CHC Handlooms was born from a deep respect for the rhythm of the loom. In a world of fast-moving trends, we chose to slow down, returning to the tactile warmth of natural cotton and the enduring beauty of handcrafted drapes.

Founded in Sarojini Nagar Market, New Delhi, our journey is intertwined with the city's rich history of textile commerce—a legacy we carry forward into the modern Indian home.

"Affordable living is not about the price tag; it's about the feeling of soft cotton on a quiet afternoon, and the story woven into every thread."

Our Philosophy

Designed for
Slow Living

We believe your home should be a sanctuary—a place where every texture invites touch and every corner feels thoughtfully curated. Our philosophy is simple: tactile living, breathable materials, and everyday elegance.

Every bedsheet, curtain, and rug we produce is designed to foster a sense of calm, bringing the timeless affordability of the handloom into the contemporary living space.

The Craftsmanship

Authentic Artistry

Our process begins with the selection of the finest natural fibers. From the rhythmic movement of the shuttle to the careful hand-finishing of each piece, our artisans bring generations of expertise to the craft. We don't just make furnishings; we preserve a way of life.

The Anchor

A Legacy at
Sarojini Nagar

Located at 23A, Sarojini Nagar Market, New Delhi, CHC Handlooms is more than a store. It is a heritage anchor in one of India's most iconic textile hubs.

For decades, this location has served as our center for craftsmanship and community, where we connect directly with families to understand how they live, sleep, and style their homes.

The Modern Indian Home

Timeless Textures,
Today's Aesthetic

While our methods are rooted in tradition, our aesthetic is firmly contemporary. We design for the modern Indian family—those who appreciate the richness of heritage but demand the clean lines and breathable comfort of 21st-century design.

Earthly affordability, neutral tones, and mindful textures come together to create homes that are as beautiful as they are lived-in.
